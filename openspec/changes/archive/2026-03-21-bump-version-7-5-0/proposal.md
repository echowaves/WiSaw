## Why

Preparing for the next release after updating 21 Expo packages to SDK 55-compatible versions and unifying the close button component. The version bump from 7.4.15 to 7.5.0 reflects these improvements, and the build number increments from 539 to 540 for the new build.

## What Changes

- Update `version` from `"7.4.15"` to `"7.5.0"` in `package.json`
- Update `buildNumber` from `"539"` to `"540"` in `package.json`
- Update `versionCode` from `539` to `540` in `package.json`
